Ideas for improvments to Project 2

  • up to 12 pts. Change the rules of the L-system to get interesting plants. Look at the real plants outside for ideas. Use buds, conditional rules, random rules, etc.
  • up to 10 pts. Use a few iterations of a Bezier curve to get curved leaves, stems, fruit, or flowers.
  • up to 7 pts. Use randomness (man random for more on generating random numbers) in your drawing functions, to get more interesting and realistic looking plants.
  • up to 3 pts. Vary the colors and use color interpolation on your polygons to get a more natural effect.
  • up to 10 pts. Make a few different kinds of plants and group them together to make a landscape.
